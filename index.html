Simulation of Blue-green Algae Blooms Drift in Dianchi Lake

Map the blue-green algae bloom area retrieved from remote sensing at the initial time into grid particles, and then calculate the particle migration coordinates under dynamic conditions according to the particle migration equation, generate the time-sharing bloom grid layer of the whole Dianchi Lake, and render it in layers, that is, convert the point set data into grid data for visualization.

Projects of Kunming Environmental Monitoring Center

Visualization of Chl-a Concentration in Dianchi Lake

On the basis of the discrete point grid, taking into account the wind speed and direction, the inflow and outflow of the lake, and other factors, read the initial concentration data of Chl-a, use the model to calculate the concentration value at each time, and use VTK to realize the visual expression of the concentration data.

Projects of Kunming Environmental Monitoring Center

Visualization of Flow Field in Dianchi Lake

Considering the factors such as wind speed and direction, and runoff in and out of the lake, the model is used to calculate the flow field. The calculation results of the abstract flow field data are displayed in vector arrow graphics. By setting the radius, length, color, and other attributes of the arrow, it is used to represent the direction and velocity of the flow on the grid point.
